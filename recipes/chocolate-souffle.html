Chocolate Souffle

Description

This is my own take on the classic french dessert chocolate souffle

It is not overly sweet

It has just the right amount of sugar and the perfect consistency a chocolate souffle should have

It is also gluten free

Ingredients

  • Chocolate
  • Butter
  • 3 eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Chop up your quality chocolate, then melt it with butter in a double boiler or in 20 second increments in the microwave
  2. Separate your eggs. It's convenient that this recipe uses 3 egg whites and 3 egg yolks there's no extra yolks or whites
  3. Whisk egg yolks, vanilla, and salt into your chocolate/butter mixture
  4. Whip egg whites and cream of tartar into soft peaks
  5. Slowly add/beat in the sugar
  6. In 3 additions, slowly fold the beaten whites into the chocolate mixture
  7. Refrigerate batter as you preheat the oven
  8. Spread or brush every crevice/inch of your ramekin with softened butter, then coat in granulated sugar
  9. Spoon batter into ramekins, then level off with a knife or flat icing spatula
